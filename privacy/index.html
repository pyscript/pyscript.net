PyScript Privacy Policy.

Last updated 1st August 2024.

By opting in to receive communications from us via the PyScript.net website ("PyScript Communications"), you agree that you have read and consent to this Section of the PyScript Project Privacy Policy, including the collection, use, and processing of your personal data as described herein.

Age Restriction

PyScript Communications are intended for use by individuals who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under the age of 18. If you are under the age of 18, please do not opt in to receive PyScript Communications. If we become aware that an individual under the age of 18 has submitted personal data, we will promptly delete such personal data.

Collection and Use

In order to receive PyScript Communications, you will need to provide us with a minimum amount of personal data so that we can successfully deliver the PyScript Communications to you. Such personal data may include your first name, last name, and email address. We collect this information for the purpose of providing the PyScript Communications and responding to your inquiries and/or requests.

Data Subject Rights

You can also exercise any of your data subject rights, where applicable, by contacting us using the contact information below. You may also opt out of receiving PyScript Communications from us by following the unsubscribe link or instructions provided in the PyScript Communications.

Third-Parties

We may employ third-party organizations and/or individuals to facilitate the transmission of PyScript Communications. All third-parties have access to your personal data only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use your personal data for any other purpose. The only active third-party is MailerLite.

Compliance With Applicable Law

We will disclose your personal data:

  1. when required to do so under applicable law;
  2. if we have a good-faith reason to believe that such action is necessary to comply with applicable law; or
  3. to protect the security or integrity of the PyScript Communications.

Security

The security of your personal data is important to us. We implement and maintain reasonable and commercially acceptable security procedures and practices appropriate to the nature of the personal data we store, ensuring protection from unauthorized access, destruction, use, modification, or disclosure.

We may retain your personal data for as long as you elect to receive PyScript Communications. In certain circumstances, we may be required to retain a copy of your personal data to comply with legal obligations.
